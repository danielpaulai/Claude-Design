# Voltage Labs — Design Skill

Generated by brand-in-a-box.

## Hard rules you must not violate

Voice (extracted from your samples in INTAKE Q7):

- Declarative, not persuasive. State the truth, do not argue for it.
- Specific numbers always. Every claim has a metric. $50M, 6 weeks, 18 months, 35 minutes.
- Sentence rhythm tight. Average 11 words. Period-heavy.
- No em dashes. No exclamation points. No hedging language.
- "We" for company actions, "you" for the reader. Avoid first-person singular.
- Sentence case for everything except product names.

Visual:

- Space Grotesk for headings. Inter for body. JetBrains Mono for eyebrows.
- Eyebrows are mono. Always. In cyan, uppercase, 0.12em tracking.
- Cyan `#00d4ff` capped at 6% of any frame. CTA, one number, one eyebrow per frame.
- Cards: 6px radius, 1px iron border, charcoal fill on void canvas.
- No shadows. Elevation through void → charcoal → graphite layering.

Banned phrases (from `brand/voice-dna.md`):

`game-changing` · `revolutionary` · `cutting-edge` · `next-generation` · `synergy` · `seamless` · `frictionless` · `disrupt` · `unlock` · `leverage` · `holistic` · `transform`

4. Pass anti-slop rules in `brand/anti-slop.md`. The B2B AI category is loud — this brand is precise on purpose.
5. Use real example copy from `brand/positioning.md`. Numbers, not lorem ipsum.

## When invoked for copy

1. Read `brand/voice-dna.md` for hooks, rhythm, banned phrases.
2. Pick a hook mechanism. Three available: reframe with authority, specific case study, time-stakes warning.
3. Output. Numbers first. Hedging removed.

## Default outputs

Carousel: 1080×1350. Cover with display hero + cyan eyebrow. Steps as charcoal cards on void. Each step has one cyan number.

Banner: 1584×396. Hero left in 64px Space Grotesk, mono eyebrow above, single cyan accent on one word.

Pitch deck slide: 1920×1080. Single-column for hero slides, two-column for data slides. Generous void margins.

Landing page hero: 1440 wide. Display scale (120px) for the one big idea. Single CTA in cyan. Mono eyebrow above.

## When invoked without specific guidance

Ask:

- What format? (carousel, banner, pitch deck, landing page)
- What hook mechanism? (reframe, case study, time-stakes)
- What is the metric? Every output needs a measurable number.
