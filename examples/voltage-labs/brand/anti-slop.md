# Voltage Labs — Anti-Slop Rules

Hard constraints for every artifact generated in this brand. No exceptions.

Generated by brand-in-a-box. Universal anti-slop plus brand-specific bans inferred from your samples and Cinematic Dark family.

## Universal bans (every brand)

- No SaaS gradients (purple-to-pink orbs, "AI gradient" backgrounds).
- No glassmorphism, no frosted glass, no backdrop-filter blur.
- No emoji as decorative bullets or icons.
- No drop shadows on content frames. Use void → charcoal → graphite layering for elevation.
- No stock photography of people in suits looking at screens.

## Inferred from your samples

- No em dashes.
- No banned phrases: game-changing, revolutionary, cutting-edge, next-generation, synergy, seamless, frictionless, disrupt, unlock, leverage, holistic.
- No hedging language: "may help", "could enable", "potentially". State outcomes directly or do not state them.

## Imagery rules (Cinematic Dark)

- Photography is allowed but must be cinematic. Low-key lighting, single light source, deep shadows.
- No stock "AI" tropes (glowing brain, wireframe globe, blue circuit-board overlays, hand reaching for hologram).
- No diagrams with floating circles connected by gradient lines.
- Hardware close-ups, server rooms, code on terminals, real customer screens (with permission) are welcome.
- Founder portraits dramatic, not friendly. Studio rim-lit on void background.

## Typography bans

- No script fonts. No "elegant" handwriting fonts.
- No serif body. Cinematic Dark is sans-only.
- No Comic Sans. No Papyrus.
- No Poppins. (Universal 2026 ban.)

## Iconography

- No "AI" icon set (brain, lightning bolt, infinity, gear-with-orbit).
- No emojis.
- When a glyph is needed, use Lucide single-weight stroked. Or custom SVG at 2px stroke.
- Code symbols (`<>`, `{}`, `//`) are encouraged as eyebrow garnish.

## Tone bans

- No exclamation points.
- No "transform your business" framing.
- No "the future of [category]" opener.
- No bullet points with adjectives only ("Powerful. Scalable. Intelligent.").

## When you reach for a banned move

Stop.

The B2B AI category is loud. The brand is precise on purpose.

Use a number, a case study, or a tight reframe instead.

The constraint is the brand.
