# Marigold & Oak — Colors

Generated by brand-in-a-box. Warm Editorial family.

Three brand colors. Cream and ink for everything else.

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Terracotta | #c8543b | Primary accent. Headlines, CTAs, highlight words. Warmer than red, less aggressive. Cap at 8% of any frame. |
| Ink | #1a1814 | Body text, dark sections. Has a slight brown undertone, never pure black. |
| Sage | #7d8a73 | Secondary accent. Tags, soft dividers, secondary CTAs. |
| Warm white | #fbf8f3 | Default canvas. Warmer than off-white. |
| Cream | #f4ede4 | Card backgrounds, subtle section breaks. |
| Stone | #9a8e7e | Muted text, captions. |

The 8% rule on terracotta is more generous than Editorial Minimal allows red.

Warm Editorial earns more accent because its baseline is softer.

Use terracotta on:

- One headline phrase per frame.
- Inline emphasis on a word that carries the sentence.
- The CTA button.
- The eyebrow on a featured section.

Never use terracotta for body paragraphs or long captions.
