# Marigold & Oak — Typography

Generated by brand-in-a-box. Family default for Warm Editorial.

## Families

Heading: Crimson Pro (semibold for warmth, bold for emphasis).

Body: Inter.

Both free on Google Fonts. Loaded from CDN in `colors_and_type.css`.

## Hard rules

The serif heading is the brand. Do not substitute a sans for headlines.

Body must stay sans for legibility on long-form pages.

Body size 17px minimum. Warm Editorial reads more relaxed than Editorial Minimal — the bigger body is intentional.
