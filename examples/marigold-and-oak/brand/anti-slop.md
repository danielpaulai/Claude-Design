# Marigold & Oak — Anti-Slop Rules

Hard constraints for every artifact generated in this brand. No exceptions.

Generated by brand-in-a-box. Universal anti-slop plus brand-specific bans inferred from your samples and Warm Editorial family.

## Universal bans (every brand)

- No SaaS gradients (purple-to-pink orbs, "AI gradient" backgrounds).
- No glassmorphism, no frosted glass, no backdrop-filter blur.
- No emoji as decorative bullets or icons.
- No drop shadows on content frames. Use cream-on-warm-white contrast for elevation instead.

## Inferred from your samples

- No em dashes. Your samples contain zero. Treat as hard ban.
- No hashtags in social copy.
- No banned phrases: unlock, leverage, growth hack, 10x, scale fast, crush it, boss babe, level up, boss up, manifest (used loosely).

## Imagery rules (Warm Editorial)

- Photography is allowed and encouraged, unlike Editorial Minimal.
- Naturalistic, golden-hour light. Hands, plants, paper, ceramic, fabric textures.
- No stock-photo "wellness" tropes (woman doing yoga in white at sunset, hands on coffee mug, lavender in mason jar).
- No watercolour illustrations. No leaf icons. No "sacred geometry" decorative elements.
- Founder portraits welcomed. Shot warm, not dramatic. Window light over hard studio light.

## Typography bans

- No script fonts. No "elegant" handwriting fonts.
- No all-caps body copy.
- No serif body. Heading is the only place serif goes.
- No Comic Sans. No Papyrus.

## Iconography

- No "wellness" icon set (lotus, om, mandala, leaf, water drop).
- No emojis.
- When a glyph is needed, use Lucide single-weight stroked.

## Tone bans

- No exclamation points except in genuine surprise quotes.
- No "namaste" sign-offs.
- No "with love and light" or similar wellness-coded sign-offs.
- No present-tense statements that sound like affirmations ("I AM abundance," "She IS the brand").

## When you reach for a banned move

Stop.

The wellness category is loud. The brand is quieter on purpose.

Use a typographic, compositional, or photographic move instead.

The constraint is the brand.
