# Marigold & Oak — Ideal Client Profile

Generated by brand-in-a-box from INTAKE Q3, Q4, Q5.

## Who we serve

Female founders running $50K to $500K wellness, yoga, retreat, or holistic health businesses where the brand is an extension of the founder's values.

More specifically:

- Solo founders or 2-3 person teams
- Service-based (retreats, coaching, body work) or product-light (small candle line, wellness consumables)
- 2-7 years in business
- Founder-led brand. The founder's name and face are on the website
- Decision-maker is the founder. No committees, no spouse-as-CFO

## Where they are today (extracted from your pain in Q4)

Their brand looks like a Pinterest mood board, not a business. They keep changing it.

In practice this looks like:

- A homepage that has been "in progress" for 8 months
- Three colour palettes saved in Coolors, none of them shipped consistently
- A logo redesigned four times in two years, none of them quite right
- Captions that take 45 minutes to write because she "isn't sure if it sounds like me"
- Pinterest boards titled "brand vibes 2026 v3 FINAL"

## Their deeper pains

- "I sound different on every platform."
- "I can describe my work to a client in 30 seconds. I cannot describe my brand in 30 seconds."
- "I am a multi-passionate founder and my brand reads as scattered."
- "I do not want to look like every other wellness brand on Instagram."
- "I want my work to feel grounded. My brand feels like a craft fair."

## The transformation they want (extracted from your promise in Q5)

A coherent brand and content engine in 6 weeks. One that grows without their daily input.

This means:

- A folder they can open and know what to do
- Content that comes out in their voice without 45 minutes of agonising
- A look that other wellness founders cannot copy because it is grounded in HER values, not a trend
- Permission to stop changing it

## What they will pay for

- Done-with-you brand systems with a clear handover
- 6-week containers, not retainers
- Workshops or intensives that produce a working asset
- Brand audits with specific, opinionated direction (not "here are 12 options, you pick")

## What they will not buy

- Logo-first packages
- Generic "brand strategy" decks without an asset
- Loud, aggressive, "boss babe" coaching
- Anything that uses "manifest" as a verb in marketing copy

## Geography and channel

Primarily US, UK, Australia, Canada. English-speaking. West Coast US over-indexes.

Discover via Substack, Instagram, podcast features in the wellness business space. Convert in DMs after 4-8 weeks of warming via content.

## Disqualifiers

- Pre-revenue founders with no offer in market
- Anyone who wants a logo without strategy
- Founders building scaled, productised businesses (not our lane)
- Anyone whose brand voice is built around aggression or hustle
