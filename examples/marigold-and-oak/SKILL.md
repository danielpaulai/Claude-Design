# Marigold & Oak — Design Skill

Generated by brand-in-a-box.

Read `README.md` first. It indexes everything in this skill.

## Hard rules you must not violate

Voice (extracted from your samples in INTAKE Q7):

- Stories before claims. Open with a moment, a person, a specific detail.
- Sentence rhythm flows. Average 18 words. Let sentences breathe.
- No em dashes.
- No exclamation points except in genuine quotes.
- "I" + "you" + "she/he" mix. Third-person allowed when narrating client stories.
- Sentence case for everything except proper nouns.

Visual:

- Crimson Pro for headings. Inter for body. Serif heading is non-negotiable.
- Body 17px minimum. Line-height 1.65. The brand reads relaxed.
- Terracotta `#c8543b` capped at 8% of any frame. Use on one phrase, one CTA, one eyebrow per frame.
- 8px corner radius on cards. 1px warm-gray-300 borders. No black borders, no 0px corners.
- Cream on warm-white for elevation, not drop shadows.

Banned phrases (from `brand/voice-dna.md`):

`unlock` · `leverage` · `growth hack` · `10x` · `scale fast` · `crush it` · `boss babe` · `level up` · `boss up` · `manifest` (used loosely)

## When invoked for a visual artifact

1. Load `colors_and_type.css`.
2. Use the canvas size from `brand/tokens.json` matching the requested format.
3. Generate static HTML the user can open directly.
4. Pass anti-slop rules in `brand/anti-slop.md`. The wellness category is loud — this brand is quieter on purpose.
5. Use real example copy from `brand/positioning.md` (no lorem ipsum).

## When invoked for copy

1. Read `brand/voice-dna.md` for hooks, rhythm, banned phrases.
2. Pick a hook mechanism. Three available: universal-truth opener, client story, gentle reframe.
3. Output. Stories first. Claims second.

## Default outputs

Carousel: 1080×1350 PDF, 8-12 slides. Cover serif hero, lessons in cream cards, CTA close.

Image post: 1080×1350 JPEG. Single moment. Big serif headline, naturalistic photo or full-bleed type.

Article header: 1080×607. Hero serif left, optional photo right. Eyebrow in sage or terracotta.

One-pager: A4 @ 300dpi. Editorial column grid. Hero top, lead in serif, supporting columns in sans.

## When invoked without specific guidance

Ask:

- What format? (carousel, image post, article header, one-pager)
- What hook mechanism? (universal truth, client story, gentle reframe)
- Is there a specific client story to ground the piece in?
