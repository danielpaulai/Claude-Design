# Hook Library

Every hook in the content library, extracted as the first line of each post, categorized by mechanism, ranked by engagement score (likes + 3×comments + 5×shares).

## How to use

- Picking a hook for a new post? Find the mechanism that matches the post's intent, then steal the structure of the top-performing hook in that category.
- Writing in bulk? Aim for 5-6 different mechanisms across a week so the feed does not feel repetitive.
- Avoid hooks that are not in this file. If a hook does not fit any mechanism here, it probably is not in Daniel's voice.

## Mechanism definitions

- **Transformation / I-built.** "I built X in Y and got Z result." Concrete, outcome-first. Establishes authority by showing the work.
- **Vulnerability / Personal story.** A confession, a memory, a moment of doubt. Breaks the LinkedIn-bro pattern. Earns trust fast.
- **Contrarian observation.** "I have seen X, and it is wrong." Plants a flag. The reader has to agree or disagree.
- **Category reframe.** "In 2025 you needed X. In 2026 you need Y." Redraws the map so we are the default answer.
- **Advice / If-then.** "If I had to start again..." or "If you are doing X, stop." Direct, useful, skimmable.
- **Urgency / Event promo.** Tied to a workshop or deadline. Use sparingly; too many of these and the feed feels like a sales channel.
- **Stat-drop / Intrigue.** Open with a number or a surprising fact that forces a second line read.
