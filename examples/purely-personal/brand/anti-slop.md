# Purely Personal — Anti-Slop Rules

Hard constraints for every artifact generated in this brand. No exceptions.

Generated by brand-in-a-box. Universal anti-slop plus brand-specific bans inferred from your samples and Editorial Minimal family.

## Universal bans (every Editorial Minimal brand)

- No SaaS gradients (purple-to-pink orbs, "AI gradient" backgrounds).
- No glassmorphism, no frosted glass, no backdrop-filter blur.
- No emoji as decorative bullets or icons.
- No stock photography. No generic vector illustrations of business people.
- No "rounded-3xl" puffy cards.
- No drop shadows on content frames. Elevation is borders and contrast.

## Inferred from your samples

- No em dashes. Your samples contain zero. Treat as hard ban.
- No hashtags in LinkedIn copy.
- No banned phrases: growth hack, 10x, leverage, unlock, secret sauce, guru, hustle, synergy, disrupt, game-changer, follow for more.

## Imagery rules

- No stock photography.
- No generic illustration of business scenarios.
- No SaaS gradients or blob shapes.
- Founder portraits only. Shot moody, shadow-side lit, near-black backdrop, desaturated.

## Typography bans

- No Poppins. No Comic Sans. No decorative or hand-drawn fonts.
- No system-ui in shipped artifacts.

## Iconography

- No emoji in UI labels, buttons, or post text.
- No generic icon sets (Font Awesome, Material Icons, Feather) used decoratively.
- No unicode pictographs (★, ✓, →) as decorative elements.
- When a glyph is needed (repost, chevron, check), substitute Lucide. Single-weight, stroked.

## When you reach for a banned move

Stop.

Use a typographic or compositional move instead.

The constraint is the brand.
