# Purely Personal — Colors

Generated by brand-in-a-box from your INTAKE.

Three brand colors. Everything else is a neutral.

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Primary Red | #e90d41 | Accent only. CTAs, eyebrow pills, highlight words, arrows. Cap at 5% of any frame. |
| Near Black | #0a0a0a | Body text, dark backgrounds, card borders. Never pure black. |
| Silver | #b8bec1 | Secondary text, hairline borders, muted UI. |
| Off-White | #f7f7f8 | Paper canvas. Full-bleed in cheatsheets and one-pagers. |
| White | #ffffff | Card interiors on off-white canvases. |

The 5% rule is the difference between editorial and shouty.

Use red on a verb, a number, or a CTA. Never on a paragraph.
