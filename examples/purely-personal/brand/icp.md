# Purely Personal — Ideal Client Profile

Generated by brand-in-a-box from INTAKE Q3, Q4, Q5.

## Who we serve

Solo founders running $100K to $5M coaching or service businesses where the founder is the brand.

More specifically:

- Founders, CEOs, and consultants where the founder is the face
- Service-based, knowledge-based, or coaching businesses
- Decision-maker and buyer in the same person (no committees, no procurement)

## Where they are today (extracted from your pain in Q4)

They post on LinkedIn for 12 hours a week and nobody books a call.

In practice this looks like:

- Inconsistent posting, then two-week silences when life gets busy
- Pipeline goes quiet every time they go quiet
- Have Claude or ChatGPT open but produce average output
- Re-explaining themselves to a tool that should already know them
- Doing five jobs manually that AI could handle

## Their deeper pains

- "My content gets likes but nobody books a call"
- "I am paying for AI tools that are not making me any money"
- "I know I need to post consistently but I do not have the hours"
- "Every week I re-explain myself to a tool that should already know me"

## The transformation they want (extracted from your promise in Q5)

90 days of on-brand content in two hours a week, not twelve.

This means owning a system that:

- Writes in their voice without being prompted from scratch
- Knows their ICP and offer cold
- Ships faster than they can manually edit
- Stays sharper over time, not flatter

## What they will pay for

- Done-with-you systems they own and run themselves after setup
- Workshops that produce a working asset by end of session
- Personal brand builds that reliably attract inbound high-ticket leads
- Claude Skills customised to their voice, ICP, and offer

## What they will not buy

- Generic AI consulting or "prompt packs"
- Content agencies that own the voice
- Tool recommendations without implementation
- Theory without a working outcome in the room

## Geography and channel

Primarily English-speaking founders: US, UK, Canada, Australia, EU, Singapore.

Discover via LinkedIn content and live workshops. Convert in DMs or on a short call after engaging with content.

## Disqualifiers (not our ICP)

- Enterprise buyers with procurement cycles
- Non-founder employees looking for career tools
- Anyone looking for the cheapest option
- Anyone who wants to outsource their voice entirely
