# Purely Personal — Positioning

Generated by brand-in-a-box from INTAKE Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4, Q5.

## One-line positioning

I build founders an AI-native content engine that turns LinkedIn into an inbound client channel.

## Category

LinkedIn-first personal branding for founders, powered by AI content systems.

Not a content agency. Not a ghostwriter. Not an AI consultant.

Inferred anti-positioning: a new category that sits at the intersection.

## Who we serve

Solo founders running $100K to $5M coaching or service businesses where the founder is the brand.

See [icp.md](icp.md).

## The pain we solve

I post on LinkedIn for 12 hours a week and nobody books a call.

## The promise

90 days of on-brand content in two hours a week, not twelve.

## Why us instead of the alternatives (inferred)

| Alternative | Why founders choose us instead |
|---|---|
| Content agency | We do not hold their voice hostage. They own the system after handover. |
| Ghostwriter | The writing sounds like them, not like a generic LinkedIn creator. |
| AI consultant | We produce a working asset in the room, not a strategy deck. |
| "Just use ChatGPT" | We build the system around their ICP and voice so it gets sharper over time, not flatter. |
| Doing it themselves | We compress 12 hours a week into two. |

## The shift we are selling

From: the founder typing questions into Claude and getting average answers, re-explaining themselves every day.

To: AI running alongside the founder. Marketing handled, sales followed up, operations moving. The founder staying where they should be, doing the work only they can do.

## Enemies (inferred from the pain in Q4)

- Generic AI output that sounds like every other founder on LinkedIn
- Tool-of-the-month overwhelm
- Content theatre. Posting for vanity metrics instead of pipeline
- Agencies and ghostwriters who own the founder's voice

Direct. Confident. Outcome-first. Plain English.
