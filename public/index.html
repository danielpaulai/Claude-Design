01

02

        

          
Make a 7-slide LinkedIn carousel

          
Replace [INSERT TOPIC] with your idea. Paste into the same Claude Design chat. Watch 7 slides render on the canvas in your brand.

          

            

              Prompt 02 — Topic to Carousel
              
            

            
═══ MISSION ═══

Topic: [INSERT TOPIC]
Outcome: One LinkedIn carousel that earns me the inbound DM from my ICP.

═══ STRUCTURE ═══

7 slides on canvas. 1080×1350 each. Stack vertically.

Slide 1 — COVER. Hook in 8-12 words. Eyebrow pill, big type, accent on ONE word. Footer module if my brand uses one.

Slides 2-6 — LESSONS. Each slide does ONE job: eyebrow "STEP N" + lesson title (5-9 words) + body (2 sentences max) + one visual element. Vary the visual move slide to slide.

Slide 7 — CTA. Soft, sounds like a DM not an ad. "Comment [WORD] and I'll DM you the [asset]." Below: P.S. that asks a question.

═══ COPY DISCIPLINE ═══

- Match my sentence rhythm within ±2 words
- Use 1-2 of my ranked hooks (contrarian, transformation, category reframe)
- Include one specific number with a timeframe
- Banned phrases auto-rejected

═══ SELF-CHECK BEFORE DELIVERY ═══

☐ Banned phrases anywhere? ☐ Accent used >1× per slide?
☐ Slide-1 hook and slide-7 CTA in same voice register?
☐ Sentence rhythm matches my voice rules?
☐ Slide 1 hook promises what slide 7 delivers?
☐ Any AI tells (gradient, generic illustration, em dash)?

═══ AFTER DELIVERY ═══

Tell me which 2 slides are weakest, offer to remix.
Suggest 3 follow-up topics for this series.

          

          

            What you should see
            7 slides rendered on canvas at 1080×1350 each. Cover, 5 lessons, soft CTA. All in your brand. About 90 seconds.
          

        

      


      
      

        
03

        

          
Reverse-engineer a winning ad

          
Drop a screenshot of an ad you admire (Stripe, Hormozi, Linear, Notion) into the chat. Then paste this prompt. Claude decodes WHY it works, then ships 3 variations in your brand.

          

            

              Prompt 03 — Winning Ad to My Version
              
            

            
═══ INPUT ═══

[ATTACH IMAGE OF THE AD]

Industry context: [my industry]
Why I think this works: [one sentence — your hypothesis]

═══ STEP 1: FORENSIC DECODE ═══

Critique the ad like a designer. Tell me in chat (don't render yet):

Composition — Where does the eye land 1st, 2nd, 3rd? Negative space ratio?
Copy — Hook structure? Headline word count? CTA explicit or implicit?
Visual signature — Color hierarchy? Type contrast? The ONE move that makes this premium / trustworthy?
Emotional beat — What feeling lands? What objection does it preempt? Anti-audience?

═══ STEP 2: BRAND TRANSLATION ═══

Now render my version on canvas. Same skeleton, my brand.

Take from the original: composition, copy structure, emotional beat, negative space.
Replace from my brand: colors, fonts, voice, imagery, accent placement.

═══ STEP 3: VARIATIONS ═══

Render 3 variations side by side, original's exact dimensions:
  V1. FAITHFUL — closest mapping
  V2. CONFIDENT — push my brand's signature gesture harder
  V3. RISKY — invert one decision (dense → sparse). Brave version.

═══ STEP 4: VERDICT ═══

After rendering, answer:
1. Which variation would YOU ship and why?
2. Predict A/B test ranking (original vs my V1-V3).
3. What did the original do that I should NOT copy?

          

          

            What you should see
            A short forensic decode in chat, then 3 variations rendered side by side on canvas. Each one in your brand, using the original's structure.
          

        

      


      
      

        
04

        

          
Borrow a Pinterest layout (never the colors)

          
Drop a Pinterest image (magazine cover, A24 poster, album art). Pick something opposite to your brand for maximum effect. Paste this prompt. Get 2 variations in your brand using the reference's structure.

          

            

              Prompt 04 — Pinterest to My Layout
              
            

            
═══ INPUT ═══

[ATTACH PINTEREST IMAGE]

What I want to build: [carousel cover / image post / one-pager / banner / pitch slide]

═══ EXTRACTION TARGETS ═══

In chat first, tell me:

The grid — asymmetric / symmetric / modular? Alignment lines? Columns?
The focal point — what pulls the eye? How is it framed? Focal-area ratio?
Typography hierarchy — hero/body size ratio? Number of distinct styles?
Density rhythm — crowded → empty? Where does the eye rest?

═══ APPLY TO MY BRAND ═══

Render the artifact in MY brand. Structure borrowed, everything else mine.

Forbidden imports: colors, fonts, imagery, mood (use my brand's).
Required from reference: grid, focal-point framing, negative space ratio, type hierarchy ratio.

═══ OUTPUT ═══

Render two variations side by side:
  V1. FAITHFUL — structure 1:1, brand applied
  V2. LOOSENED — structure simplified by 20%, more breathing room

═══ THE TASTE TEST ═══

Before rendering, ask yourself: "If I stripped colors and fonts, would a designer see the same skeleton in my V1 and the reference's wireframe?"

If yes → nailed it. If no → redo.

          

          

            What you should see
            A short structural breakdown in chat, then 2 variations rendered on canvas. Both in your brand, both using the Pinterest reference's composition.
          

          

            Reference picks that always work: Wallpaper, Kinfolk, or Cereal magazine covers. A24 movie posters. Frank Ocean's Blonde album cover. Vintage Müller-Brockmann Swiss design posters.
          

        

      


      
      

        
05

        

          
Iterate to refine (don't restart)

          
After any artifact, when 80% is right and 20% needs adjusting. Use this prompt in chat — or use Claude Design's inline comments on the canvas. Both work.

          

            

              Prompt 05 — Iterate to Refine
              
            

            
═══ INPUT ═══

The artifact you just rendered: [name it]

═══ MY DIRECTION ═══

Want to push:
  + [thing to amplify]
  + [thing to amplify]

Want to soften:
  - [thing to dial back]

Want to try:
  ? [experiment, optional]

Keep untouched: [list anything sacred]

═══ EXECUTION ═══

Step 1: Acknowledge. Repeat back what you're amplifying / softening / trying.
        If anything is ambiguous, ask ONE question.

Step 2: Render V2 on canvas next to V1 (split view, same canvas size).

Step 3: In chat, give me a 60-word "diff explained" — the specific decisions and why.

═══ DIFF DISCIPLINE ═══

A real iteration changes 3-5 things, not 30. If you're rebuilding half the artifact,
you're starting over. Pause and ask if that's what I want.

═══ AFTER ═══

Two questions:
1. Ship V1, ship V2, or run another iteration?
2. If we iterate again, what's the smallest next change?

          

          

            What you should see
            V1 (your previous version) and V2 (the iteration) side by side on canvas. A 60-word diff in chat below.
