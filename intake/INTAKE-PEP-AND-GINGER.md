# Brand In A Box — Intake (filled example: Pep & Ginger)

This is the filled intake for a Playful Color brand.

Use it as a reference if your brand sits in consumer products, food and drink, or community-driven categories.

This is also the live demo intake used in the workshop. Paste this into Claude with the brand-in-a-box kit loaded and watch a complete brand generate in about 90 seconds.

---

## 1 · The brand

**1. Business name.**
Answer: Pep & Ginger.

**2. One-line description.**
Answer: I make small-batch ginger drinks for people who quit coffee and refuse to drink kombucha for the rest of their lives.

**3. Who you serve, in one sentence.**
Answer: People aged 28 to 45 who are caffeine-curious or caffeine-sober, who buy from Whole Foods or DTC, and who want a drink that tastes like an actual drink.

---

## 2 · The transformation

**4. The pain you solve.**
Answer: Every "healthy energy drink" tastes like cough syrup and costs $8.

**5. The promise.**
Answer: A drink that tastes like an actual drink. Real ginger, real lemon, real fizz. $4 each, ships monthly.

---

## 3 · How it should look and sound

**6. Pick one aesthetic family.**
Answer: Playful Color.

**7. Three writing samples.**

Answer (paste below):

```
We don't do "wellness." We do drinks. There's a difference.

If your morning needs three apps, four supplements, and a Pinterest mood board to function, I cannot help you. I can give you a 6oz bottle of ginger and lemon and the hour back you spent picking outfits for your gut bacteria.

Started Pep & Ginger in January after I quit coffee and discovered the entire "healthy energy" aisle was either kombucha or anxiety in a can. Two years later we are a team of four, shipping 8000 bottles a month from a kitchen in Lisbon.
```
